About PrismPuzzle

Experience the ultimate sliding puzzle game with premium wood aesthetics and smooth 60FPS gameplay. Solve puzzles with numbers or your own photos.

Features

  • Multiple difficulty levels (3×3 to 7×7)
  • Number and Photo styles
  • Multiple game modes
  • Real-time achievements
  • Offline gameplay
  • 100% privacy - no data collection

Quick Start Guide

  1. Select your preferred style (Number or Photo)
  2. Choose board size (3×3 to 7×7)
  3. Pick a game mode
  4. Tap Play to start
  5. Drag tiles to slide them
  6. Solve the puzzle!

Controls

Dragging

Tap and drag a tile to move it. All connected tiles slide together.

Buttons

Shuffle: Randomize the board (before first move)

Undo: Revert last move

Redo: Forward to next move

Hint: Show next move suggestion

Pause: Pause the game

Puzzle Rules

Solvability

All puzzles are guaranteed to be solvable.

Scoring

Your score is based on moves and time taken. Fewer moves and less time = higher rating.

Star Ratings

Earn up to 3 stars based on your performance compared to your personal best.
